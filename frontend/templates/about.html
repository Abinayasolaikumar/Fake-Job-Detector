About RecruitShield AI

Deep learning defense against fake recruitment emails.

Why Detect Fake Recruitment Emails?

Recruitment fraud is a rapidly growing attack vector where attackers pose as legitimate employers to steal personal data, collect money, or compromise devices. These scams often exploit job seekers' urgency and optimism, combining social engineering with convincing branding.

Detecting these threats early protects candidates from identity theft, financial loss, and long-term reputational damage. Organizations can also use these insights to harden their talent acquisition channels and communication policies.

A cyber defense shield continuously scanning recruitment emails for high-risk anomalies.

How the AI System Works

  1. Preprocessing & Normalization – Incoming emails are cleaned using NLP: lowercasing, tokenization, stopword removal, lemmatization, and special character handling.
  2. Contextual BERT Embeddings – Each token is embedded using a pre-trained BERT model to capture deep semantic context from recruitment-related phrases.
  3. Hybrid Detection Engine – A hybrid CNN + LSTM + Attention network learns both local n-gram patterns (e.g. payment language) and long-range dependencies (e.g. offer structure).
  4. Risk Scoring & Explainability – The system produces fake/genuine probabilities, maps them to a risk band, and surfaces linguistic and domain-based signals as human-readable reasons.
  5. Audit & Reporting – Every analysis is stored in a secure SQLite-backed repository, powering dashboards, trends, and forensics.
